McDollibee

McDollibee is a global fast-food corporation born from the merger of McDonald's and Jollibee in 2025. Headquartered in Manila-Singapore, it operates in over 100 countries, blending Western and Asian culinary traditions to create a unique dining experience. Known for its innovative menu, vibrant branding, and cultural inclusivity, McDollibee serves millions daily, revolutionizing the fast-food industry by bridging cultures and flavors worldwide.

SERVICES

Dine in

McDolibee offers a variety of American-influenced fast food items and casual Filipino fare, including Chickenjoy (fried chicken), Yumburger, McJolly Spaghetti, and Palabok Fiesta.

Delivery

McDolibee provides food delivery services, allowing customers to order online through the McDollibee mobile app, the McDollibeeDelivery.com website, or the #123321 hotline.

Takeout

Customers can also order food for takeout from Jollibee restaurants.

Menu

Jollibee's menu includes Chickenjoy, Burgers, Pies, Meal Deals, Perfect Pairs, Chicken Sandwiches, Chicken Tenders, Jolly Spaghetti, Palabok Fiesta, Burger Steak, Family Meals, Sides, Specialty Drinks, and Drinks and Desserts.
